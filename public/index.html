About ROC & AUC

The **Receiver Operating Characteristic (ROC)** curve plots the **True Positive Rate (TPR)** against the **False Positive Rate (FPR)** at various classification thresholds.

The red dashed line represents a completely random classifier (AUC = 0.5). A perfect classifier hugs the top-left corner, achieving an **Area Under the Curve (AUC)** of 1.0.

*Note: Tree-based outputs are binary nodes, showing single-point thresholds, whereas KNN and Logistic Regression yield full probabilistic distributions.*
